Why Aussies Love Carnival Cruises: The Ultimate Family Cruise Experience

Date: 8 June 2025

What Makes Carnival Cruises So Popular in Australia?

  • Affordable Family Deals: Carnival is famous for competitive fares and frequent sales, so families can often snag a bargain—especially when booking early or watching for last-minute offers.
  • Aussie-Friendly Destinations: With itineraries covering the South Pacific, Queensland, Tasmania, New Zealand, and short “fun cruises,” there’s a perfect voyage for every family.
  • Fun For Everyone: From kids’ clubs (with age-specific programs) to adults-only Serenity retreats, everyone gets their own slice of holiday bliss.

Standout Features For Aussie Families

  • Waterparks & Pools: The Green Thunder waterslide (the fastest and steepest at sea!) is legendary with kids and teens.
  • Endless Activities: Mini golf, movies under the stars, family trivia, comedy shows, and deck parties—no one gets bored!
  • Dining for Every Taste: Heaps of choices, including Aussie BBQ, pizza, and specialty restaurants.
  • Spacious Family Cabins: Options for 3, 4, or even 5 guests in one room, plus interconnecting cabins for bigger families.

Carnival vs. P&O and Royal Caribbean

While P&O is popular with many Aussies, Carnival edges ahead for sheer fun factor, American-style service, and lively onboard energy. Royal Caribbean brings even more wow-factor (ice rinks, surf simulators), but often at a higher price. Carnival’s value and fun-first approach make it a family favorite.

Tips for Booking a Carnival Cruise

  • Book Early For School Holidays: Family cabins sell fast during Christmas, Easter, and spring breaks.
  • Check for Promotions: Look for “kids sail free” or onboard credit deals—these come up often.

FAQ

Q: What’s included in a Carnival cruise fare?
A: Accommodation, most meals, onboard activities, entertainment, and kids’ clubs are all included. Specialty dining, drinks, excursions, and WiFi cost extra.

Q: Is Carnival good for first-time cruisers?
A: Absolutely! Carnival’s relaxed vibe, easy check-in, and heaps of family-friendly activities make it ideal for cruise newbies.

Q: Can I cruise from my city?
A: Carnival departs from Sydney and Brisbane year-round, and offers occasional sailings from Melbourne.
