Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is Happy Cruiser?
Happy Cruiser is an independent, Aussie-owned travel platform for families, bringing you expert advice and the best affiliate cruise, hotel, flight, and travel insurance deals—so you can save time, money, and stress!
👩‍💼 Are you a travel agent?
Nope! We're an online resource and booking comparison site. When you book, you’re sent directly to our trusted cruise, hotel, or insurance partners, often with family-exclusive offers.
💰 How does Happy Cruiser make money?
We earn a small commission when you book via our links—no extra cost to you. It keeps the site free and independent for Aussie families.
🚢 Do you only recommend certain cruise lines?
We compare all the major lines but love family favorites like Carnival and Royal Caribbean, with a special focus on cruises from Australia.
🔒 Is my data safe?
Yes! We only collect your email if you sign up for updates or download a checklist. Your info is never sold. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
🧑‍💻 Can I contact you for help?
Absolutely! Visit our Contact page—we love helping Aussie families plan unforgettable holidays.
📄 Other popular questions
  • Do I need a passport for all cruises? – For domestic cruises, sometimes no! But always check your itinerary and cruise line.
  • Are meals included? – Yes, most meals and snacks are included. Specialty restaurants cost extra.
  • Can we book family cabins? – Yes, Carnival and Royal Caribbean have cabins for up to five. Book early for best options.
  • What about travel insurance? – Highly recommended! Get a quote when you book through Happy Cruiser.
